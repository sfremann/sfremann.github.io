Hello there!

I’m a developer with a strong background in computer science and mathematics, passionate about RPGs and narrative games, as a creator and as a gamer. In my free time, I like writing dark fantasy stories with unnecessarily complex characters and lore. I also like when it’s fun. Everyone needs a good laugh once in a while, even in a dark and depressing world.


Being both an artist and a programmer with a slightly maniacal obsession for problem-solving and mathematics, I’ve always struggled to decide whether to do creative or technical work. I also just figured out I could create some nice tools to help integrate creative content into games such as animation, cinematic or VFX – anything to help fellow game developers and artists to bring high-quality games to life, with compelling and authentic storytelling.

Projects


Dialogue tree plugin for Unreal Engine

🚧 In progress Tool UE5 C++ Python

Plugin adding a dialogue tree editor in Unreal Engine 5 for future personal game projects. The dialogue tree asset is made of custom nodes using story flags to check or set, text, audio, animation and camera position. The plugin also handles how dialogue is supposed to play in-game when speaking with characters.


Highlights:

  • Dialogue tree editor.
  • In-game dialogue tree asset behaviour.
  • Additional ProjectSettings to create, save and load custom story flags.
